History Of

There is no debate over the influence that Japanese Animation has had on the world, not only film but in games and media in general. The history of anime to our current knowledge can be traced back to the start of the 20th century.
Without going in too deep on how Walt Disney Productions and early Japanese Animators where inspired by each other, and how each medium evolved on their respective own it is in my opinion a certain fact that "Anime" has great influence over many genres and mediums.
There are many anime titles of Anime that has reached great levels of acclaim and many that has caught widespread attention within their respective genres. 
Below are a only a few(at least two) whose titles(in order of release) alone you should know :
Astro Boy originally released in 1963 by Osamu Tezuka, widely revered as the Father of Anime as it is know today. It was one of the first anime series to be released reaching world wide fandom, inspiring so many of the anime & manga artists whose works we have today.
Gundam originally released in 1979 not the greatest of releases of its time, with that said it has a massive fan base, especially after later realeases within the same timeline of the original story arc.With Legend Of The Galactic Heroes paved a road for future titles within the "Mecha" genre.
Dragonball/DBZ originally released in 1986 reaching immediate success to the level where it is still one of the best selling Manga in current Japan & Abroad.
